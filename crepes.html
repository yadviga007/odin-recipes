Tasty crepes as a desert

Description

Crepes are a great idea for a dish. They are versatile, so they can go with anything. I really recommend them as a desert though ;).

Ingredients

  1. 2 eggs
  2. 1 cup milk
  3. 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  4. 1 pinch salt
  5. 1,5 teaspoons vegetable oil
  6. TIP: add 0,5 glass of sparlinkg water
